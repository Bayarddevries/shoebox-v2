Uncle Johnny

Johnny Cousineau as remembered by Gail Hargreaves:

"He worked for CN. He repaired. Whatever in the train needed repairing? And. When the queen came to the Paw, he was on that train just to make sure everything was functioning properly. 

He was a Avid Hunter and Fisher. Fisherman, they my dad only hunted Birds, but Johnny hunted everything. Yeah. And it was not unusual to go to his garage and see a deer hanging in it. Yeah, and Grandpa the same thing. There'd always be ducks hanging in his shed.

So, when I came to Winnipeg, we came when I was in grade five. Friend down the street. Oh, look. They have a deer in their garage, but yeah. So, what's wrong with that?"
